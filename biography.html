Biography

I’m Racheal Yema Okwudike, but you might know me as Rayspotlight. Music has always been a part of me, but my journey started as a professional dancer. After graduating from the University of Port Harcourt, Nigeria, in 2020, I decided to fully step into my true passion—music.

Blending Afrobeats with hip-hop, I’ve built a loyal following and gained recognition for my unique sound. My style is inspired by the legends who paved the way—Nicki Minaj, Cardi B, Burna Boy, Wizkid, and Foxy Brown—artists who’ve shaped my love for music.
